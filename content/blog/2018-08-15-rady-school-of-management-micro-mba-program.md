Micro-MBA from UCSD's Rady School -- "a great opportunity for graduate students and postdocs with technical backgrounds to be introduced to business concepts." Inspired by *The Ten-Day MBA* by Steven Silbiger.

Recognized ambitions beyond pure research -- combining scientific expertise with business acumen. Key transferable skill: "my ability to simply explain difficult ideas." Employers will increasingly value employees who can translate technical complexity into accessible language.
