Describes transformative journey. Began taking college courses while incarcerated in 2007. Committed to pursuing Ph.D. in Physics despite not having completed calculus. Achieved doctorate after years of dedicated work.

"I could not have made it this far without the help of family and friends. As I reflect on the mentors that offered guidance when I had little, I hope to provide guidance to others while remaining humble enough to still be guided myself."

Journey: "from a GED to a Ph.D."
