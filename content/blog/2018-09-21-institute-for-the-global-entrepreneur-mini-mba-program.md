After completing Micro-MBA, enrolled in more intensive Mini MBA from Institute for the Global Entrepreneur at UCSD. Curriculum emphasized collaborative exercises, building competencies in strategic thinking, financial acumen, team science, market insight, and operational execution. Culminated in capstone pitch to mock investors.
