Scheduled to present at 2020 APS March Meeting in Denver on critical branching behavior in digital memcomputing machines. Conference canceled due to COVID-19. Created recorded presentation.

Research on memcomputing -- "efficient computing paradigm that uses memory to process and store information in the same physical location." Examines digital memcomputing machines solving 3-SAT.
