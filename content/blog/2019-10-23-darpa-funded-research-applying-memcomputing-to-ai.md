Di Ventra Group at UCSD received $500,000 in DARPA funding to investigate physics-based computational methods applied to AI. Research concentrates on memcomputing techniques during unsupervised learning for training Deep Belief Networks.

**Links:** UCSD press release "Riding the Third Wave of AI without Quantum Computing"
