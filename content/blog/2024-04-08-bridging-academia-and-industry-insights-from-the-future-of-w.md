Describes attendance at UC San Diego's Future of Work conference. Collaborated with Jiaqi Liu from Gilead Sciences to discuss bridging academic theory with workforce demands, emphasizing problem-solving, adaptability, and curiosity.

Key recommendations for students:
- Aligning academic projects with job market requirements
- Pursuing professional certifications
- Participating in industry conferences and competitions

"A relentless pursuit of knowledge and solutions is the most valuable asset a professional can offer."

**Alt:** Speaking with students at UCSD's Future of Work Conference.
