Attended 2019 SACNAS conference in Honolulu to represent UCSD. Received email "Are you The Storyteller?" and submitted narrative about spending years in prison and now completing PhD in physics.

Erin Barker (Artistic Director) collaborated on stage presentation. "A cathartic experience" demonstrating "people can change their lives no matter the circumstances."
