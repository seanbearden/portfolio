## 10. Scientific Reports: Efficient solution of Boolean satisfiability problems with digital memcomputing
**Date:** November 12, 2020

Announces publication in Scientific Reports. Introduces memory-assisted physical system (digital memcomputing machine) demonstrating polynomial scalability for hard SAT instances.

The physical system solves SAT in continuous time without requiring chaos or exponentially growing energy. Efficiency from collective dynamical properties persisting during numerical integration.

**Image:** circle_circuit.png
**Alt:** A digital memcomputing machine realized as self-organizing logic circuit.
